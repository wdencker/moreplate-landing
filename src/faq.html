Frequently Asked Questions

  • How easy is this to set up?

    Your restaurant can be up and running using Moreplate within a day. Just send us your menu in any format—we'll take care of the rest.

  • How much does this cost?

    Due to the disproportionate effect COVID has had on the restaurant industry, we are offering the Moreplate product for free. We just ask that you give us honest and consistent feedback (so we can make the product better!).

  • Do I need to train my staff to use your software?

    No software training is required. Moreplate shows your staff each order in real-time. You can think of it as the digital equivalent of a guest check (except much better, of course).

  • I'm already using ChowNow / Uber Eats / DoorDash / Olo / etc. to take pickup or delivery orders. How is this different from that?

    These apps are built for pickup and delivery, not in-restaurant dining (for instance, they do not allow your customers to submit orders at different times throughout a meal before paying once at the end). Moreplate is used exclusively for in-restaurant dining, designed to make the digital sit-down experience seamless and intuitive for both your customers and your staff.

  • What about the POS I’m currently using? Is this meant to replace that?

    Moreplate integrates seamlessly into your current POS and ticketing service so you are able to run your back-of-house and tech stack as it already exists.

  • How do I ensure that customers ordering alcohol are of legal age?

    If a customer orders an alcoholic beverage with Moreplate, they will be prompted to upload a photo of their identification that is easily verified.

  • How do I get paid?

    We use Stripe to process payments and are able to remit funds in real-time to your restaurant account.

  • How do I edit my menu?

    Everything you need to handle menu changes is available in the dashboard. Easily add photos, edit prices, and promote items with one click.

  • How do I receive orders?

    Whatever is easiest for your kitchen. Depending on your setup, Moreplate can either place orders into your POS automatically or allow your staff to enter them as usual.

  • How do I distinguish orders between different tables?

    When you sign up your restaurant with Moreplate, our dashboard creates a QR code and table number graphic for each of your tables, which you can print out and place wherever you'd like. Then, each order you receive is easily identified by the table that placed it. Table numbers and configurations can be updated at any time in the dashboard.
